## Decision

Use exactly one level of semantic folders: `_system/`, `decisions/`, `sessions/`, `knowledge/`, `tasks/`.

All further categorization happens in frontmatter fields, not deeper folders. Dataview queries filter by frontmatter, not by path traversal.

## Alternatives Considered

1. **Deep nesting** — fragile paths, requires tree knowledge to navigate
2. **Flat + tags only** — can't scope Dataview queries efficiently, everything is one bucket
3. **Zettelkasten IDs** — designed for human serendipity, not machine retrieval
