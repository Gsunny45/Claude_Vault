**vmmem/vmmemWSL explained (write once, read forever):**
- `vmmemWSL` = the WSL2 Linux virtual machine. Cannot be killed. IS WSL2. Not a problem.
- `vmmem` (non-WSL) = Docker Desktop's Hyper-V VM. Now removed from startup.
- Task Manager "End Task" on these = access denied by design. They are hypervisor processes owned by SYSTEM.
- After next clean boot: vmmemWSL ~800MB-1.2GB idle, vmmem GONE.

**Current baseline after fixes:** ~8.8GB used / 6.6GB free. Acceptable.
